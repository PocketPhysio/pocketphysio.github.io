PRESCRIBE WELL

Video Exercise Programs

PocketPhysio helps you make exercise programs for your patients that continue to teach them after they leave their appointment. By having a video playlist or handout resource on hand, your patients can improve their at-home exercise technique to recover faster.

SAVE TIME

Create Programs Fast

PocketPhysio allows you to find the exercises you want, add them to a program, and share it with your patient, all within a couple of minutes. This stunningly simple process saves you from scribbling notes and drawing stick figures, giving you more time to focus on your patient.

ACCESSIBLE

Rehab Anywhere

After you share the program with your client, they receive an email inviting them to a page where they can view exercises individually or start their rehab with a one-touch-play, all this on any device, from anywhere. PocketPhysio removes obstacles for your patients recovery.
